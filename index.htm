About SESC

Who We Are

Established on April 29, 2013, at the Faculty of Sciences (UFAS1), Setifian Scientific Club (SESC) is the first scientific club in Setif. We are a dynamic community dedicated to fostering intellectual growth, bridging theory with practice through seminars, workshops, and field expeditions, and empowering students to excel academically and beyond.

More than an academic forum, SESC is a close-knit community that nurtures creativity, fosters friendships, and develops community awareness. We provide internships, job opportunities, and a platform for students to realize their potential with our slogan: "Together We Realize Our."
